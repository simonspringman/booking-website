Executive Summary

Three critical updates for board awareness — Q1 2026.

Digital Platform Complete

Full booking platform prototype operational with 22 API endpoints, 19 destination pages, admin dashboard, AI chatbot, wallet system, and 7 payment methods. Ready for production migration.

Revenue Performance

RWF 456M total revenue in Q1 2026 (▲18% QoQ). Digital channels (website + mobile) now represent 70% of bookings, up from 53% in Q3 2025.

Amadeus Migration On Track

5-phase migration plan to Amadeus Digital APIs in progress. Phase 1 (Core Booking) at 35% completion. Target go-live: July 2026 (Q3).

Key Takeaway: The digital transformation is delivering measurable results: conversion rate up 2.1pp to 13%, AI chatbot resolving 80% of inquiries, and mobile bookings growing 30% quarter-over-quarter.
