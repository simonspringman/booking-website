Everything you need to know before you fly with RwandAir.

Excess Baggage

Extra bags can be purchased during booking or at the airport. Pre-purchase online to save up to 40%.

Restricted Items

Sharp objects, flammable materials, and liquids over 100ml are not permitted in hand baggage.

Travel Documents

Passport

Your passport must be valid for at least 6 months beyond your travel date. Ensure the name on your booking matches your passport exactly.

Visas

Check visa requirements for your destination before travel. Many African countries offer visa-on-arrival or e-visa options. Visit irembo.gov.rw for Rwanda visas.

Health Requirements

Some destinations require a Yellow Fever vaccination certificate. Check the latest health advisories before you travel.

Check-in

Online Check-in

Opens 24 hours before departure and closes 3 hours before. Get your boarding pass on your phone or print it.

Airport Check-in

Airport counters open 3 hours before departure for international flights and close 1 hour before.

Boarding

Gates close 30 minutes before departure. Please arrive at the gate on time.

Special Assistance

Passengers with Disabilities

Wheelchair assistance and special seating can be arranged. Please request at least 48 hours before departure.

Unaccompanied Minors

Children aged 5-12 travelling alone are supervised by our cabin crew throughout the journey. Fee applies.

Travelling with Pets

Small pets may travel in the cabin in an approved carrier. Larger animals can be transported in the cargo hold.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change or cancel my booking?

You can modify or cancel your booking through the "Manage Booking" section. Enter your booking reference and last name to access your reservation. Changes and cancellations are subject to fare rules and may incur fees depending on your fare type. Economy Classic fares can be changed with a fee, while Business Class fares offer full flexibility.

What is the baggage allowance?

Economy Lite includes 7 kg hand baggage only. Economy Classic includes 7 kg hand baggage plus 1 checked bag (23 kg). Business Class includes 7 kg hand baggage plus 2 checked bags (32 kg each). Extra baggage can be purchased online during booking at a discounted rate.

How early should I arrive at the airport?

For international flights, arrive at least 3 hours before departure. For domestic flights, arrive at least 2 hours before. Check-in counters close 1 hour before departure for international flights.

Can I select my seat in advance?

Yes! Economy Classic and Business Class passengers can select their seat for free during booking. Economy Lite passengers can purchase seat selection as an add-on. Seat selection is also available during online check-in.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Amex), Mobile Money (MTN and Airtel Money), and bank transfers. Mobile Money is the most popular payment method for bookings originating from Rwanda.

How do I request a refund?

Refund eligibility depends on your fare type. To request a refund, contact our call centre or submit a request through the "Help" section. Business Class fares are fully refundable. Economy Classic fares may be refundable with a fee. Economy Lite fares are non-refundable.
